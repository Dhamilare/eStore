Common Queries

We offer standard and express delivery options. Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days, while express delivery arrives within 1-2 business days. Delivery times may vary based on your location and product availability. You can track your order status in your account.

We accept returns of unused and undamaged items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange. Custom or personalized items are generally non-returnable. Please visit our Contact Us page to initiate a return.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a DHL tracking number. You can use this number on our Order History page or directly on the DHL website to monitor your shipment's progress.

Yes, we do! We ship to a wide range of international destinations. Shipping costs and delivery times for international orders vary depending on the destination country and the size/weight of your order. You can see the exact shipping cost at checkout.

We securely accept various payment methods through Paystack, including major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve) and bank transfers. All transactions are processed securely.

You can reach our customer support team via our Contact Us page, by emailing us at support@ecomstore.com, or by calling us during business hours at +123 456 7890. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
